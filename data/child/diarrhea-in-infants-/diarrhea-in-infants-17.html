Diarrhea in Infants

Your baby's diarrhea may be caused by too much fruit juice in the diet. The sugars in fruit juice can be hard for your baby to digest. Try decreasing the amount of juice and other sweet drinks in your baby's diet. Babies do not need fruit juice and generally should not drink more than four ounces of juice per day.
