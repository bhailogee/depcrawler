Diarrhea in Infants

Your baby's diarrhea could be a response to eating a new food, for example, due to a food allergy or a problem digesting certain types of foods.

Signs and symptoms of allergy or digestion problems may include stomachache, gas, vomiting, fussiness, rash or wheezing. If your suspect your baby's diarrhea is due to an allergy, stop giving the food in question and call your doctor.
