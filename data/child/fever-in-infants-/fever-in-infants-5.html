Fever in Infants

Babies under three months who have a fever need to see their doctors immediately.

Let's ask some more questions to help us understand where your baby's fever could be coming from.

Does your baby have a runny nose, cough, or sore throat? (Babies obviously can't tell you they have a sore throat, but they may drool more than usual, point to their mouths, or not want to eat or drink.)

Yes, my baby has one or more of those symptoms.

No, my baby does not have one or more of those symptoms.
