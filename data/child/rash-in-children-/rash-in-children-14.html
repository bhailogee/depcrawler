Rash in Children

Your child's rash does not have pus-filled blisters or red bumps covered by a honey-colored crust. A rash that looks like that could be impetigo, a bacterial infection of the skin.

Does your child's rash consist of raised, red bumps or welts? Do they seem to move around on the skin? Do they itch or hurt?
