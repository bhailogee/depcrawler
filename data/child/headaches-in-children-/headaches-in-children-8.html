Headaches in Children

Are any of the following true?

- Your child sometimes sees lights or has other changes in his or her vision before getting the headaches

- Your child sometimes feels nauseated or vomits with the headaches

- When your child gets the headaches, lights and noise bother him or her

- Your child suffers from carsickness

- Family members have been diagnosed with migraine headaches

Yes, one or more of these is true.

No, none of these are true.
