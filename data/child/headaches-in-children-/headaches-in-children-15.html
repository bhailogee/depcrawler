Headaches in Children

As is the case with adults, headaches in children can be triggered by lack of sleep, poor eating habits, and dehydration.

Try to get your child to sleep, eat, and drink better -- it's important for general health, not just headache prevention!

If the headaches continue, or get any worse, call your doctor.
