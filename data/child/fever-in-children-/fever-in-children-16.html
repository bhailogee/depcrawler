Fever in Children

Does any of the following describe your child?

  • He is vomiting green liquid (bile) or blood.

  • He can't keep anything in his stomach.

  • His belly looks very big.

  • He is in a lot of pain.

  • There is blood or mucous in his stool.

  • He hasn't urinated in more than six hours.

  • His mouth is dry, or he seems very weak or sleepy.

  • He has a rapid heartbeat or rapid, shallow breathing.

  • His skin is pale or bluish.
