Nutrition Assessment for Infants and Toddlers

For women who choose not to breast-feed for medical or personal reasons, infant formula contains all the nutrients that a baby needs for normal growth. Babies should continue drinking formula, and should not start drinking cow's milk, until 12 months of age.
