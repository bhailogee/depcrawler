Favorite fruit suggestions for young children include: apples, bananas, nectarines, peaches, pears, oranges, grapefruits, and strawberries. Older children may also enjoy cantaloupe and other melons, mango, kiwi, plums, and dried fruits such as apricots, dates, prunes, and raisins.

Remember that whole grapes and cherries should not be given to toddlers because they are a choking hazard.
