Car Seats

Children who are at least one year old and weigh between 20 and 40 pounds should ride forward facing, preferably in the back seat. They can sit in a convertible seat or a forward-facing car seat.

Click below to learn more about these types of car seats.

If your car only has lap belts, travel vests may be an option if your child has outgrown his car seat but isn't big enough for a booster seat.
