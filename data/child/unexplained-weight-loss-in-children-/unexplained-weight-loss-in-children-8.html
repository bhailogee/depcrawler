Unexplained Weight Loss in Children

Excessive thirst and urination with weight loss can be symptoms of diabetes. It's good that your child isn't showing these signs.

Along with the weight loss, has your child had any of the following?

- A rapid heartbeat

- Tremors

- Swelling in the front of the neck

- Eyes looking bigger, as if they are "popping out"
