Unexplained Weight Loss in Children

Call your doctor now.

Illness is one of the most common reasons for weight loss in children, both because the fever burns up extra calories, and because children eat less when they are sick.

However, any illness that is serious enough to cause weight loss warrants being checked by a doctor. He or she will decide if further tests or treatments are needed.
