Rash in Newborns

Your baby's rash doesn't fit the description of the most common rashes in newborns.

While most rashes in newborns are not cause for concern, it is a good idea to figure out what exactly is causing the rash.

Call your doctor to describe the rash and decide whether and when a visit to the office makes sense.
