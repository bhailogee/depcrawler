Diaper Rash

Your baby's rash does not seem to fit the description of the more common diaper rashes.

If your baby's rash is extensive and bothering him, you should call your doctor for an appointment so that he or she can take a look at it. If it is a mild rash, try changing your baby's diaper frequently and use plenty of diaper rash cream. If it gets worse, or if it's not improving in two or three days, call your doctor.
