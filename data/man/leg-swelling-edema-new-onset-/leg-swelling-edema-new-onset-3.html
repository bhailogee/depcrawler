Leg Swelling (Edema), New Onset

Almost all women have some leg swelling during pregnancy. If you are a pregnant woman, please visit our guide on Leg Swelling during Pregnancy.

Otherwise, please continue with the guide. Even if you have one cause of swelling in both legs, there may be additional causes. We suggest you follow the guide until the end to learn about common causes of leg swelling.
