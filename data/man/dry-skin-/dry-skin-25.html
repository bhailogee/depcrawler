Dry Skin

Less common reasons for dry skin are vitamin and mineral deficiencies. These can arise because of:

- Rapidly losing a lot of weight

- Active cancer, especially if you're undergoing chemotherapy or radiation

- Malabsorption syndromes such as celiac disease and chronic diarrhea

- Gastric bypass surgery

- Poor nutrition
