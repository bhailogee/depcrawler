Fever in Adults

If your ear hurts and you have a fever, you could have an ear infection.

An infection of the inner ear is called otitis media; an infection in the outer part of the ear is called otitis externa (or swimmer's ear). Antibiotics are not always necessary for this, but it's a good idea to get checked by your doctor.
