Hand Pain

Thank you for using this guide. We hope your hand pain gets better quickly but be sure to contact your doctor if it does not.

And remember, contact your doctor right away if

  • you cannot use your hand

  • your symptoms developed after a significant injury

  • you have a fever

  • your hand is red or swollen.
