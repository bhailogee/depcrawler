Numbness or Tingling

Your symptoms could be due to a pinched nerve in your neck. This can occur just due to aging (with arthritis and disc degeneration), or it might develop due to an injury, whether recent or remote. Contact your doctor if the symptoms worsen or persist for more than two weeks.
