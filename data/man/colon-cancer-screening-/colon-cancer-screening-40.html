Colon Cancer Screening

Uncommon types of polyps, such as hamartomas, juvenile polyps and inflammatory polyps are almost always benign. People who are found to have only one of these types of polyps generally do not need special follow-up. Your doctor can advise you on a specific plan for follow-up testing.
