Lumps or Pain Within the Scrotum

Fortunately, pain in the scrotum is rarely a sign of a serious problem such as cancer.

When the discomfort is in the top portion of the scrotum, near the groin, likely causes include a hernia, pinched nerve, or strain of a ligament or muscle.
