Hair Loss in Men

Hair loss that follows a medical illness or another type of stress is quite common. It is usually due to a condition called telogen effluvium.

Within the past 6 months, have you had or experienced any of the following?

- A serious medical illness or major surgery

- Loss of a large amount of weight
