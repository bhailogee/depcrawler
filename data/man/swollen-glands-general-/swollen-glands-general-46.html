Swollen Glands (General)

If you have unusual discharge or a sore on your genitalia, you may have an infection. Since your lymph nodes have been swollen for a few weeks already, call your doctor today to arrange evaluation.

You should also avoid sexual contact until you have talked to a doctor about your symptoms.
