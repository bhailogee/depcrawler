Swollen Glands (General)

If there are swollen lymph nodes in areas beyond your armpit, your immune system is reacting to a more widespread rather than a local problem. You may have swollen lymph nodes in other areas of your body that you are not aware of.

If there are other symptoms that you are having, your doctor will use these as clues to help diagnose the cause of your generalized lymphadenopathy.

Do you have any of the following symptoms

  • recurrent or persistent fevers

  • joint pains

  • rashes on your skin

  • unusual fatigue?
