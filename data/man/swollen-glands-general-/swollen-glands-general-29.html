Swollen Glands (General)

So far you have indicated that you have felt one or more swollen lymph nodes, but none in your groin or armpit. If you only feel them in your neck, please visit our Health Decision Guide titled Swollen Glands in the Neck.

It's rare to have a swollen lymph node that is not in the neck, groin or armpit. A couple of rare spots are just above either elbow and in the navel (belly button). If you do think you have a swollen lymph node in an unusual place and it has persisted this long, contact your doctor.
