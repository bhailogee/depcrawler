Swollen Glands (General)

Contact your doctor. You may have an infection in the foot or leg that is activating your body's immune system and causing you to have a swollen lymph node.

This is especially important if

  • you have diabetes

  • you are having fevers with these symptoms

  • you have a red line going up your leg from the injury toward the lymph node.

Contact your doctor's office today.
