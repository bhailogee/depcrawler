Swollen Glands (General)

Lymph nodes in the groin are at the top of a lymphatic channel that goes all the way down to the toes. An infection in the toes, legs or genitalia can cause swelling in the lymph nodes of the groin.

Do you have any of these symptoms

  • cuts or sores on your feet or legs

  • areas of redness on the feet or legs

  • new swelling in the feet or legs?
