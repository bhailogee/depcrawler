New Onset Dizziness

Your answers suggest that the problem is more likely a temporary inner ear disorder. The likely possibilities are

- Benign positional vertigo

- Labyrinthitis

- Vestibular neuritis

Try to control the symptoms by remaining still.

If the symptoms are severe or you develop vomiting and can't hold down fluids, call your doctor or proceed directly for urgent care.

If the symptoms persist, even if not severe, call your doctor's office for advice.
