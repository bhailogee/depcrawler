New Onset Dizziness

Some people say they are dizzy when they just have a feeling of being "out of it" -- like a head full of cobwebs that you can't clear. Or you may have a continuous lightheaded feeling that remains even if you lie down.

Many medications can potentially cause this type of dizziness as an unwanted side effect.
