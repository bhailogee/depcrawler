Smell and Taste Loss

Okay. It's quite possible that your trouble tasting things is related to exposure to a medication or an airborne toxin. Be sure to talk to your doctor about this -- your sense of smell may improve if you stop or change one of your medicines or if you avoid exposure to the airborne toxin.

But, be sure to check with your doctor before making any changes to your medicines.
