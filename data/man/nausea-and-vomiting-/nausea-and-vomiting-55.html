Nausea and Vomiting

You are reporting several symptoms, including nausea or vomiting, fever, and vertigo. The diagnosis that most closely matches your symptoms is a viral infection within your balance control center near to your inner ear. This type of infection is called "labrynthitis." Labrynthitis is not a dangerous condition, but it is quite uncomfortable. Your doctor may be able to prescribe medication to minimize your nausea and other symptoms if this is determined to be your diagnosis.
