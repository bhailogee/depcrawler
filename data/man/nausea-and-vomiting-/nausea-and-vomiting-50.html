Nausea and Vomiting

Your nausea or vomiting is very worrisome since it is accompanied by fever and pain in the right abdomen. Several dangerous infections can cause symptoms identical to yours. Seek medical care today so that a doctor can examine you.

The most concerning problems that can result in fever, right-sided abdominal pain, and nausea or vomiting include appendicitis, a gallbladder infection (cholecystitis), and hepatitis.
