Nausea and Vomiting

Your symptoms suggest in infection within the liver or its drainage system (biliary tract). This is an emergency that requires further evaluation immediately! Please come to an emergency department or other medical facility today.

Yellowing of the skin (called "jaundice") or eyes (called "icterus") can be caused by an infection within the liver ("hepatitis") or within the liver's drainage system ("cholangitis"). Fever and nausea or vomiting are common symptoms for each of these infections.
