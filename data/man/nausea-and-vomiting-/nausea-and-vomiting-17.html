Nausea and Vomiting

When abdominal pain accompanies nausea or vomiting, problems that cause weight loss are of special concern. Weight loss can suggest that you have impaired digestion (malabsorption), inadequate nutrition due to the loss of food by vomiting, or a serious illness such as cancer.
