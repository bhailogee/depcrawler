Nausea and Vomiting

You should have a thorough medical evaluation by your doctor. If a medical cause for your symptoms is not discovered, you should consider the possibility that your nausea or vomiting may be a symptom of either emotional stress or a psychiatric condition such as an anxiety disorder or depression. This is a common occurrence.
