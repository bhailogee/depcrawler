Nausea and Vomiting

Recurring nausea and vomiting can sometimes be symptoms of heart disease. Most people who are having insufficient blood flow through their coronary (heart) arteries experience chest pain or heaviness, called angina. Of course, we should think through whether heart disease is likely in your case before we consider any less serious explanations. If you are having additional symptoms that can arise from a heart attack, then you need immediate evaluation.
