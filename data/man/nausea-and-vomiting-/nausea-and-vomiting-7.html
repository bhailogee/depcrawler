Nausea and Vomiting

Problems that cause weight loss in addition to recurring symptoms of nausea or vomiting are of special concern. Weight loss can suggest that you have impaired digestion (malabsorption), inadequate nutrition due to the loss of food by vomiting, or a serious illness such as cancer.
