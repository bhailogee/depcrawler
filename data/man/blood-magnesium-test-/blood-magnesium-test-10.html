Blood Magnesium Test

Not getting enough to eat, or not getting enough of the right things to eat, can cause a low magnesium level.

 

Your doctor will probably advise you to choose more foods rich in magnesium. These include whole grains, beans, dark-green leafy vegetables like spinach and kale, nuts, and pumpkin seeds.
