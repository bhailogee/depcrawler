Blood Magnesium Test

Persistent diarrhea is one of the most common causes of a low magnesium level.

 

Treating the diarrhea should help improve your magnesium level. Your doctor may also suggest that you take a magnesium supplement.

 

In addition to your diarrhea, there other factors could by causing or contributing to your low magnesium level.
