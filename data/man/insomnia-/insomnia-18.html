Insomnia

Instead of proceeding with our guide for insomnia, you should consult your doctor about the specific physical symptom that you are noticing in the mornings.

If you have a physical condition that is being treated with medicine, then it is common for symptoms to be more active in the morning if your medicine is short-acting, since medicine can wear off during the night.
