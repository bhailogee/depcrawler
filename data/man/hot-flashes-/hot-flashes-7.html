Hot Flashes

Treatment with estrogen can prevent hot flashes, and it is more effective than other available treatments.

Although estrogen is a very reliable treatment for hot flashes, using estrogen after menopause does create some health risks. One of the most important risks from hormone replacement is breast cancer.
