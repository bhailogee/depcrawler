Pain in the Eye

One of more of the oil glands on the eyelids may become plugged and inflamed. This can cause blepharitis, a sty or a chalazion. You can start with home treatment with gentle eye cleansing and warm compresses as described for

  • blepharitis

  • styes and chalazions.

If the symptoms are not improving, call your doctor's office.
