Swallowing Difficulty

Since you have had weight loss, contact your doctor to discuss your uncomfortable swallowing.

Your symptoms and the feeling of a lump in your throat might be signs of an overactive thyroid (hyperthyroidism). The thyroid gland can swell to a larger size in a treatable condition named Graves' disease. See your doctor for an examination and blood test to check for hyperthyroidism.

Your symptoms could also come from stress and anxiety.
