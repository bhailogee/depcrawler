Difficulty Passing Urine

Contact your doctor immediately! You should have an urgent medical evaluation. Symptom such as fever, shaking chills, pain in the back or flank, or blood in the urine can be a sign of a serious infection such as acute prostatitis or pyelonephritis. Another possibility is that you may have a kidney stone.
