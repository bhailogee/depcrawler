Heart Palpitations

Some otherwise healthy people have a slow regular pulse. However, you have indicated that your pulse is irregular. This is abnormal, but not likely to be dangerous. Call your doctor's office to arrange to have an EKG done. Tell your doctor if you have noticed any other symptoms with your slow irregular pulse, such as new fatigue or decreased exercise tolerance.
