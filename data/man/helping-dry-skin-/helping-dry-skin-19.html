Helping Dry Skin

Moisturizers are a great way to aid or prevent dry skin. As necessary during the day, you should apply a moisturizer that contains at least one of the following ingredients: glycerin, urea, pyroglutamic acid, sorbitol, lactic acid, lactate salts or alpha hydroxy acids.

If your skin is very dry, you may want to apply moisturizer more frequently. For more information on treating dry skin, click on the link below.
