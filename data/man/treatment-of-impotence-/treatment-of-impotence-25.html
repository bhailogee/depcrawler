Treatment of Impotence

PDE5 drugs such as Viagra, Levitra and Cialis are very useful treatments for impotence, but some men cannot use these drugs, other men don't have a good response, and other men develop bothersome side effects. Fortunately, other treatments for impotence are available, including

  • injections

  • suppositories

  • vacuum pumps

  • surgery.

This part of the Decision Guide will help to figure out if one of these alternative treatments makes sense for you.
