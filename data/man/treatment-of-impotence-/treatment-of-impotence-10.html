Treatment of Impotence

PDE5 drugs can also interact with drugs called alpha blockers. These drugs are usually used to treat an enlarged prostate gland, or high blood pressure.

Commonly used alpha blockers include

  • Tamsulosin (Flomax)

  • Doxazosin (Cardura)

  • Terazosin (Hytrin)

  • Prazosin (Minipress)

  • Alfuzosin (Uroxatral).
