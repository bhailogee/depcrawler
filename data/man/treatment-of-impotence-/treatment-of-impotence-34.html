Treatment of Impotence

Talk to your doctor. Suppositories are available by prescription only. At present, the only suppository on the market is alprostadil (also known as the MUSE system). Your doctor should give you clear instructions about how to use this type of treatment.

Some men find that combining suppositories with other types of treatment (for example, a vacuum pump) works better than either treatment alone.
