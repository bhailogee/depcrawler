Treatment of Impotence

Contact your doctor. In order to use a PDE5 drug, you may need to stop taking all alpha blocker drugs. However, do not stop taking your alpha blocker without first talking to your health care provider.

If your doctor decides that you cannot or should not stop taking an alpha blocker drug, you may not be able to use a PDE5 drug. This will need to be decided by your doctor. However, you may still be able to use another type of treatment for impotence.
