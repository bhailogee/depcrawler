Sexual Problems in Men

Fixing the source of your fatigue or sleep problem may help your sexual drive. You may need to go to bed earlier, cut back on work or social commitments, or eliminate other things that sap your physical or mental energy.

Contact your doctor if you do not know why you are fatigued or having difficulty sleeping.
