A number of chronic medical conditions may cause a person's interest in sex to go up and down. Common medical conditions that may affect sex drive include

  • heart disease or heart failure

  • emphysema or other types of chronic lung disease

  • cancer

  • strokes or dementia

  • severe arthritis

  • inflammatory bowel disease.
