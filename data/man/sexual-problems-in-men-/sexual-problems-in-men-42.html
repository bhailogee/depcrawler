Sexual Problems in Men

Many men suffering from chronic medical conditions will notice that their sex drive goes down when their medical conditions are more active. Chronic medical conditions can affect sex drive because of fatigue, pain or discomfort, depression, and medication side effects. Fixing these problems may or may not be easy, but take the time to discuss your concerns with your physician.
