Foot Pain

Stop what you are doing andcontact your physician right awayor go to a local health facility for emergency care.

Although it may turn out to be nothing serious, your symptoms could be related to an infection in the joint, the skin or other tissues of the foot. And a foot infection is a serious matter, particularly if you have diabetes or poor circulation. If it turns out you do not have an infection, return to this guide to learn more about the most common causes of foot pain.
