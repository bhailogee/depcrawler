Wheezing in Adults

Contact your doctor today. You could have a disorder that needs prompt medical care.

There are several conditions that cause wheezing and shortness of breath at rest, including asthma, bronchitis and congestive heart failure.

After you have called your doctor, you may wish to visit our Health Decision Guide on Shortness of Breath.
