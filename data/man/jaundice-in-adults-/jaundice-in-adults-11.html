Jaundice in Adults

Statistically, one of the most likely causes of your jaundice is hepatitis (liver inflammation). There are many different causes of hepatitis.

Hepatitis can cause several symptoms in addition to jaundice. Some common symptoms include:

- Discomfort in the upper abdomen

- Nausea

- Aching joints

- Abdominal swelling

- Weight loss

- Easy bruisability and spontaneous bleeding
