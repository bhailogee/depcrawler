Jaundice in Adults

If you are a heavy drinker, your jaundice may be caused by alcohol injury to the liver.

For women, heavy drinking is more than 7 drinks per week or 3 drinks per occasion.

For men, heavy drinking is more than 14 drinks per week or 4 drinks per occasion
