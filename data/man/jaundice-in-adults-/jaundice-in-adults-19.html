Jaundice in Adults

Discuss your concern about unprotected sex with your doctor. You should be evaluated by a physician, such as your primary care doctor or a doctor in an emergency department, as soon as possible.

Hepatitis B can be diagnosed by a blood test. Your evaluation will probably begin with blood tests and a look at the liver using an ultrasound or a computed tomography (CT) scan of the abdomen.
