Diarrhea

Most people who have diarrhea have a healthy and functioning intestine lining, despite their symptoms. A healthy intestine can efficiently absorb the nutrients in your food. If you are maintaining your weight and if you efficiently digest fats in your diet (leaving no fat or grease residue in your stools), then this is good evidence that your intestine lining is healthy.
