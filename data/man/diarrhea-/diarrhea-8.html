Diarrhea

Since you are not losing weight, you are probably absorbing most of the fats, proteins, and carbohydrates in your diet. However, you may not be absorbing all of them. It is common for people to develop diarrhea when they include carbohydrates in their diet that are difficult to digest. One carbohydrate, lactose (milk sugar), can become more difficult to digest as you grow older.
