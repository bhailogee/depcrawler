Loss of Control of Urine in Men

Contact your doctor. Surgery and other types of medical procedures can damage the bladder, prostate or other important parts of the urinary tract. They can also cause infections, which may produce the same symptoms.

Your evaluation will include a urine test to see if there are signs of an infection or bleeding and possibly a visual inspection of your lower urinary tract (urethra and bladder) called cystoscopy.
