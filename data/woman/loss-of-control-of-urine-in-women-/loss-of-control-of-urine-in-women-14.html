Loss of Control of Urine in Women

These can be signs of a bladder infection. Bladder infections can sometimes be associated with urinary urgency - needing to go to the bathroom immediately. This can be a cause of losing control over your urine.

Bladder infections are treated with antibiotics. If you think you might have a bladder infection, you should contact your doctor today so that you can get the appropriate antibiotic treatment.
