Shortness of Breath In Pregnancy

Let's explore some of the causes for shortness of breath that can occur in any adult, not just a pregnant woman.

 

Let's first address whether you have symptoms of an infection, such as bronchitis or pneumonia.

 

Bronchitis or pneumonia caused by a viral or bacterial infection can make you short of breath. Typical symptoms include:

- cough, especially at night

- fever

- production of yellow or green phlegm
