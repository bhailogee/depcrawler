Shortness of Breath In Pregnancy

Even if you have a wheeze, let's explore other possibilities for your shortness of breath.

 

Another helpful clue is if you feel short of breath all the time, if it comes and goes without warning, or if you mainly feel short of breath when you exert yourself.

 

Which of the following best describes your symptoms?

I almost always feel short of breath.

I only feel it with exertion.

It comes and goes, no matter what I do.
