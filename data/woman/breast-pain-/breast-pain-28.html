Breast Pain

Clear or whitish, milky discharge may be related to:

- Pregnancy, current or recent

- Excessive, frequent stimulation of the nipples

- Side effects of medications you might be taking

- Rarely, a benign tumor in the pituitary gland within the brain

Call your doctor's office for advice.
