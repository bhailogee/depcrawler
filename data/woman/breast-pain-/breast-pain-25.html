Breast Pain

These symptoms are worrisome because they're often seen with breast infections.

Call your doctor today. You may need treatment with antibiotics. Most breast infections can be treated at home with oral antibiotics. The infection usually responds quickly. For severe infections or ones that are not getting better with oral antibiotics, hospitalization and intravenous antibiotics may be necessary.
