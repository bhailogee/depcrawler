Breast Pain

Breast pain that happens in the days before or during menses is common. Symptoms of premenstrual breast pain can sometimes be more bothersome in the years just before menopause. Try a non-prescription pain reliever, such as ibuprofen, naproxen, or acetaminophen. It can lessen or completely relieve these symptoms.

If you are not able to get relief with over-the-counter pain relievers, call your doctor's office for advice.
