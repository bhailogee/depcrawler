Insomnia During Pregnancy

Insomnia can be a side effect of medicines that stimulate your central nervous system. Here are some medicines that might be contributing to your sleeplessness:

 

- Decongestants, such as pseudoephedrine or phenylephrine (Sudafed, Sudafed PE and other brands)

- Asthma medicines (inhalers, albuterol, or theophylline)

- Prednisone
