Insomnia During Pregnancy

Your daily pattern and your sleep environment can have a major effect on how you sleep. These factors orient your brain to your schedule and condition your brain to anticipate sleep. They include:

- Your eating schedule

- Your exercise frequency, duration, and timing during the day

- Sound in your bedroom

- Light in your bedroom

- Your bedtime ritual

- Stimulation before sleep, such as reading or watching television

- Your bed or mattress

- Room temperature
