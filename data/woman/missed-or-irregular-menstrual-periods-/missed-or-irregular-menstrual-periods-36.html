Missed or Irregular Menstrual Periods

Irregular or missed periods are common at the start of menstruation. Menstrual cycles usually become regular and monthly without medical treatment within two to three years of the start of menstruation. However, contact your doctor if you have concerns or your irregular periods persist or they fail to reappear.
