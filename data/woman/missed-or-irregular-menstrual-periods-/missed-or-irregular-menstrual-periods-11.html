Missed or Irregular Menstrual Periods

You should discuss this with your doctor to see if your body needs a little more time to grow and develop, or if there is some reason for you not starting your periods.

Make an appointment with a health care professional or arrange to have someone do this for you.

Please visit our Health Decision Guide for women who Never Started Menstrual Periods.
