Frequent Urination in Women

A urinary tract infection is one of the most common reasons for frequent trips to the bathroom with only a little urine coming out. Usually women will also have some discomfort in the lower pelvic area, burning as urine passes, and an urge to go again shortly after leaving the bathroom.
