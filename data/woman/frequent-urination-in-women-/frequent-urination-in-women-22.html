Frequent Urination in Women

If you get frequent urinary tract infections, there are things you can do to help prevent these infections.

- Urinate before and after sex

- Always wipe from front to back after going to the bathroom.

- Ask your doctor whether taking an antibiotic to prevent bladder infections makes sense for you

In addition, your doctor may want to order some tests to evaluate your bladder or kidneys, looking for a reason why you experience frequent infections
