Bleeding After Menopause

You've indicated you're not sure your bleeding is vaginal. Bleeding from the genital area can be from the bladder and urethra, the vaginal area, and the rectal area. This decision guide is for bleeding from the vagina arising after menopause. Call your doctor's office to discuss your symptoms, especially since you're not sure where the blood is coming from. Be prepared to make an appointment for evaluating and managing your symptoms.
