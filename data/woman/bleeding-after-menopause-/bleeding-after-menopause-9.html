Bleeding After Menopause

Other medications can be associated with vaginal bleeding, especially medications that have hormonal activity or hormone blocking actions. For example, some drugs used in breast cancer treatment such as tamoxifen cause overgrowth of uterine tissue, known as "endometrial hyperplasia."
