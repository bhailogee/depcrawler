Hair Loss in Women

Other skin problems that can lead to scarring hair loss include:

- Fungus infections; ringworm, also called tinea capitis, is the most common infection to cause this

- Skin disorders, such as seborrhea or psoriasis

- Connective tissue diseases, such as lupus

Scarring hair loss often responds to the right medical treatment. For example, an anti-fungal medication can cure ringworm and allow hair to grow back normally.

Call your doctor's office to arrange an evaluation.
