Hair Loss in Women

If your scalp condition and hair loss started after you began using a new hair dye or shampoo, stop using the product.

See your doctor if the skin irritation does not show signs of improvement over a couple of weeks.

The regrowth of hair may not be noticeable for some time, even if the scalp condition has completely resolved.
