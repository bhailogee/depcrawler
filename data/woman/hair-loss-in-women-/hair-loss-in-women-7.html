Hair Loss in Women

You may have a "normal" pattern of hair growth and loss.

Keep in mind that the average person loses 100 hairs every day. This may be noticeable in a comb or brush, in the tub or shower, or on your pillow.

As long as you do not notice thinning of the hair on your scalp, further evaluation probably isn't needed at this time.
