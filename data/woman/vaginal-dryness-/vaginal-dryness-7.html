Vaginal Dryness

Some of these symptoms can occur during pregnancy, so a pregnancy test should be done.

Examples of medical conditions that can result in vaginal dryness at your age are premature ovarian failure, pituitary gland problems, and thyroid disease.

Please schedule a visit with your doctor. He or she can help you to think through the possible causes of your vaginal dryness and other symptoms.
