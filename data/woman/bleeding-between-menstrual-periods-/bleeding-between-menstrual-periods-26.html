Bleeding Between Menstrual Periods

Your symptoms of fever and bleeding between your periods suggest that you may have a serious infection in your uterus, called endometritis or "pelvic inflammatory disease." An infection in the uterus must be treated without delay, or there is a small chance it may result in infertility.

Contact a health professional now to arrange urgent evaluation.
