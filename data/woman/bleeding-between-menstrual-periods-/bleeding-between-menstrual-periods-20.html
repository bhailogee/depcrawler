Bleeding Between Menstrual Periods

Even though you don't have classic symptoms of a thyroid problem, your doctor will check your thyroid function as a part of your basic evaluation. Bleeding between periods can be the first symptom of thyroid disease.

Call your doctor's office to arrange an appointment. In addition to a blood test to check your thyroid function, your health care provider can update your pap smear.
