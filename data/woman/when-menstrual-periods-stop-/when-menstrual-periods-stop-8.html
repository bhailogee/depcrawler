When Menstrual Periods Stop

When a woman never has had normal periods and has noticed an excessive amount of body or facial hair, it is likely that she has a condition known as polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS). This condition affects somewhere between 5-10 percent of American women. It is usually accompanied by excess body hair, acne, and an increase amount of male hormone (androgen) in the blood.

Call your doctor's office to arrange an appointment.
