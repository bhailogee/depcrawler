Never Started Menstrual Periods

No matter what your age, if you're sexually active, pregnancy must be considered a real possibility as the cause for irregular periods. Contact a doctor, a nurse, or speak to your parents or some other responsible adult, such as a guardian or your teacher. You may be pregnant and you need to be sure that you are okay.
